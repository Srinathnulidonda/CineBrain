Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign In" button in the header and then select "Sign up" to create a new account.

How do recommendations work?

Our AI analyzes your viewing history, ratings, and preferences to suggest content you'll love.

Can I change my language preferences?

Yes! Go to your Profile settings and update your preferred languages anytime.

How do I add content to my watchlist?

Click the bookmark icon on any content card or use the "Add to Watchlist" button on the details page.
