    Cookie Policy

    Learn how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and provide personalized AI recommendations.

    Last Updated: September 22, 2025

    1. What Are Cookies?

    Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred settings and login status, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

    Important: Cookies do not contain personal information that can identify you personally, but the personal information we store about you may be linked to the information stored in and obtained from cookies.

    2. How We Use Cookies

    CineBrain uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

    • Essential Functionality: To provide core website features and ensure proper functionality
    • User Preferences: To remember your settings such as theme preference and language
    • Personalization: To deliver personalized content and AI recommendations
    • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our website and improve our services
    • Security: To protect against fraud and enhance website security

    3. Types of Cookies We Use

    Cookie Type Purpose Duration
    Essential Cookies Required for basic website functionality, user authentication, and security Session/Persistent
    Preference Cookies Remember your settings like theme, language, and display preferences Persistent (1 year)
    Analytics Cookies Help us understand website usage, performance, and user behavior Persistent (2 years)
    Functional Cookies Enable personalized features like recommendations and watchlists Persistent (6 months)
    AI Training Cookies Collect data to improve our recommendation algorithms Persistent (1 year)

    4. Specific Cookies We Use

    First-Party Cookies

    • cinebrain-theme: Stores your preferred theme (light/dark)
    • cinebrain-session: Maintains your login session
    • cinebrain-preferences: Stores your language and content preferences
    • cinebrain-analytics: Tracks anonymous usage data for improvements
    • cinebrain-recommendations: Stores data for personalized AI recommendations

    Third-Party Cookies

    • Google Fonts: Required for loading custom fonts
    • Bootstrap CDN: Essential for website styling and functionality
    • Feather Icons: Used for displaying icons throughout the site

    5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

    Browser Settings

    You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

    Browser-Specific Instructions

    • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
    • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
    • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
    • Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies and site permissions

    Note: Disabling certain cookies may impact your ability to use some features of CineBrain, particularly personalized recommendations and saved preferences.

    6. Do Not Track

    Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature that lets you tell websites that you do not want to have your online activities tracked. Currently, there is no industry standard for how to respond to Do Not Track signals, so we do not currently respond to such signals.

    7. Local Storage

    In addition to cookies, we may use local storage technologies such as:

    • Local Storage: To store larger amounts of data locally in your browser
    • Session Storage: To store temporary data during your browsing session
    • IndexedDB: To cache content recommendations and improve performance

    This data is used to enhance your experience by making the site faster and more responsive to your preferences.

    8. Cookie Consent

    By using CineBrain, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this Cookie Policy. When you first visit our website, you will see a notice about our use of cookies. By continuing to use the site, you agree to our cookie practices.

    You can withdraw your consent at any time by:

    • Adjusting your browser settings to delete or block cookies
    • Contacting us to request deletion of your data
    • Using the cookie preference center (where available)

    9. Updates to This Policy

    We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
