3.3 Billing FAQ

When will I be charged?

You'll be charged monthly on the same date you started your subscription.

Can I change my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Account Settings > Subscription > Cancel Subscription. You'll have access until the end of your billing period.

Auto-Renewal: Your subscription automatically renews each month unless you cancel before the renewal date.

4.2 Download for Offline Viewing

Available on Standard and Premium plans only

Download your favorite content to watch offline on mobile devices and tablets.

How to Download:

  1. Find the content you want to download
  2. Look for the download icon
  3. Select video quality (if available)
  4. Wait for download to complete
  5. Access from "Downloads" section

Download Limits:

  • Standard Plan: Up to 25 titles on 2 devices
  • Premium Plan: Up to 100 titles on 6 devices
  • Downloads expire after 30 days
  • Once started, finish watching within 48 hours

6. Technical Issues

6.1 Common Issues & Solutions

Buffering or Poor Quality

Solutions:

  • Check internet speed (minimum 5 Mbps for HD)
  • Close other apps using bandwidth
  • Restart your router
  • Lower playback quality in settings

Error Codes

Error 100 Network connection issue
Error 200 Content unavailable in your region
Error 300 Payment or subscription issue
Error 400 Device compatibility issue

Audio/Video Sync Issues

Solutions:

  • Refresh the page or restart the app
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Update your browser or app
  • Check HDMI cable connections (for TV)

6.2 Device-Specific Help

Web Browser

Clear cache, disable extensions, update browser

Mobile App

Update app, restart device, reinstall if needed

Smart TV

Check firmware updates, reset app, verify connection

9.2 Advanced Troubleshooting

DNS Issues

Try changing DNS servers to:

  • Google DNS: 8.8.8.8 / 8.8.4.4
  • Cloudflare: 1.1.1.1 / 1.0.0.1

VPN/Proxy Detection

Ensure CineBrain is allowed through your firewall and antivirus software.
