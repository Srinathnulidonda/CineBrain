1. Introduction

Welcome to CineBrain ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our streaming service, website, and mobile applications (collectively, the "Service").

By accessing or using CineBrain, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the Service.

Quick Summary: We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a great streaming experience, and we never sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password, and profile details
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through payment providers)
  • Preferences: Content preferences, language settings, and notification preferences
  • Communications: Feedback, support requests, and correspondence with us

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Viewing history, search queries, and interaction with content
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (for content availability)
  • Analytics Data: Performance metrics and usage patterns

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

We work with trusted third-party services for payment processing, content delivery, and analytics. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you of any such change.

8. Children's Privacy

CineBrain is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Parental Controls: We offer parental control features to help parents manage their children's viewing experience on family accounts.
